Shailja Thakur is a PostDoctoral associate at New York University. She works under the supervision of Siddharth Garg and Ramesh Karri on language models. She received her PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo in 2022, where she was advised by Sebastian Fischmeister. She received a masters and bacheleros degree in computer science from IIIT Delhi.
