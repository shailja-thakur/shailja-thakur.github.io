Shailja Thakur

I am a PostDoc at Center for Cybersecurity, Tandon School of Engineering, New York University, where I work with Siddharth Garg and Ramesh Karri. My primary research focus is on the large language models (LLMs), Cyber-Physical Systems.

I did my PhD at University of Waterloo, where I was advised by Sebastian Fischmeister. At UWaterloo, my research work was on machine learning interpretability for embedded systems, and on the analysis of side-channels for intrusion detection in real-time safety-critical systems.
