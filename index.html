About Research

Shailja Thakur

I am a Post Doctoral Research Fellow at the Center for Cybersecurity in Tandon School of Engineering, New York University, Brooklyn, NY, where I am advised by Siddharth Garg and Ramesh Karri. My primary research focuses on applying large language models (LLMs) to develop efficient, reliable, and secure cyber-physical systems with particular focus on hardware design automation using LLMs. Alongside, I work on AI fairness, trustworthiness, and privacy for cyber-physical systems.
